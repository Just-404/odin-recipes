Ripe plantain casserole

Description:

Ripe plantain casserole or better known as "pastelon de platanos maduro" is a traditional Dominican plantain-based casserole that is well-known for its combination of delicate sweetness of ripe plantains, flavorful, juicy beef filling, & melting cheese. It's a dish that is often made on the traditional Dominican Christmas feast.

Tip: Pastelón de plátano maduro is a one-pan dish, and can be served on its own, or as part of a larger meal.

Ingredients:

For the filling
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1½ teaspoons salt (or more, to taste)
  • ½ teaspoons black pepper (freshly-cracked, or ground)(or more, to taste)
  • 1 red onion, (65g), minced
  • 1 tablespoon mashed garlic, (3 large garlic cloves)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
To assemble
  • 6 ripe plantain
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup salted butter, plus extra to grease pan
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (could be any type of cheese of your preference)

Steps

For the filling
  1. Cook meat. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-low heat. Cook onion and stir it until it turns translucent. Add garlic, cook and stir for a minute. Add minced meat, cook and stir, breaking it into small clumps until it browns.
  2. Pour in tomato sauce. Add salt and pepper. Stir and cover and let it simmer for 3 minutes.
To assemble
  1. Peel the plantains and boil adding 1 teaspoon of salt to the water. Once the plantains are fork tender for 15-20 mins. Then remove them from the heat.
  2. Drain the water right away, and mash them with a fork. Add the butter and keep mashing until it is very smooth with no lumps.
  3. Layer the ingredients. Grease a 1-inch tall baking pan. Put half of the plantain mixture in the baking pan. Then cover with half of the cheese. Afterwards, cover the cheese with the meat. Cover with the remaining plantain mixture, and finally cover with the rest of the cheese.
  4. Bake. Cook in preheated oven to 350 °F [175 °C] until the top is golden brown.
  5. Serve after waiting 5 minutes after removing it from the oven so that it can cool down.
